How can I connect to NDNU wifi?

Basic Steps. (Wifi must be ON for your device)

  1. Open up any browser (Safari,Chrome,Firefox)
  2. Type in the address 10.10.100.100
  3. Proceed past any warning signs to Login page
  4. Login credentials - Username = School email username. Password = First two letters of last name + last 4 numbers of StudentID.

Where can I print documents?
  • There are three known areas to print documents.
  • The Library.
  • The Mac Lab located in St. Mary's.
  • The PC Lab located in St. Mary's.
  • You must Log-In to one of the School Computers to Print.
  • Each Document takes .10 to print. Each student has a $15 credit to print. Anything over $15 will be charged to your account.
×
What is my email login?
  • Your Student Email should be given at Registration
  • The NDNU Student Email log-in page is: https://mymail.ndnu.edu
  • Usernames are created by taking the first initial of the first name followed by the entire last name (Mary Jones = mjones). If that username is already in use, the middle initial is included (Mary S. Jones = “msjones”).
  • This username is NOT what you use to access Campus Portal. Please see the Campus Portal tab
  • Password is the same as the password used for wifi.

Where is the Commuter's Lounge?
  • There is a Commuter's Lounge located next to the cafeteria
  • It is equipped with lockers, microwave, and a refrigerator
  • Though it is very small, it is a great place to stay for some downtime
